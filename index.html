Why

Animal produced leather is expensive, toxic, and destructive at every step.
Petroleum Leather is non breathable and comparably expensive.
We offer an innovative materials solution made of simple ingredients.

  • Read More

What

Our material is made from bio-optimized SCOBY biofilm, a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast.
It is grown from water, sugar, and tea and the only waste product is kombucha, sold at $5 per 32oz

  • Read More

How

We need support to optimize our culture by adding collagen synthesis genes, giving SCOBY material the soft flexibility of leather.
